Janet Jackson's Custom Giuseppe Zanotti Sneakers for Her Tour: Details

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Shoe news! Janet Jackson is getting the custom sneaker treatment for her upcoming world tour, the Unbreakable World Tour, which begins on Aug. 31 in Vancouver, BC. 

The 49-year-old artist’s most recent tour will be her first in four years, and she’s pulling out all the stops from her head to her feet in custom-made Giuseppe Zanotti sneakers. “I am thrilled to have been asked by Janet to create the shoes for her tour,” Zanotti said in a statement. “She has been a great friend for many years, and I have the utmost respect for her as one of the most iconic and outstanding artists and style icons of our time.”

The custom kicks are a play off of one of Zanotti’s celeb-loved sneakers (that retail for a cool $1,050), but with all new color combos that match the “No Sleeep” singer’s on-stage outfits. For die-hard Jackson (or Zanotti) fans, the special edition shoes will also be available in select stores, starting in the fall.
