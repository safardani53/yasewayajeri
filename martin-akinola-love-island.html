Who is Martin Akinola?

Martin Akinola is a senior software engineer from Dublin and he already has a connection to Love Island.

The 27-year-old is good friends with summer 2022 contestant Dami Hope.

Ahead of entering Casa Amor, he has been given advice by the former cast member, including being told to "do your thing".

Talking about Dami, Martin recalled: "He said, 'Do your thing, be yourself and everyone else will get to know you, and get to see why we're friends.'
