August 2024 · 2 minute read

A WOMAN has revealed that she gave her tiny loo an epic transformation on a budget - and it’s even got posh panelling.

DIY fan Danielle, from the UK, gave her plain toilet a mega transformation, using bargain buys from Amazon and Dunelm. 

Taking to TikTok, where she regularly shares DIY and cleaning videos under the username @cleaningwithdanielled, Danielle posted a video showing off her “toilet transformation.” 

She shared her clip with the caption ‘Downstairs toilet transformation. The sink that was originally put in HAD TO GO.’ 

At the start of the video, Danielle opened the door to her toilet, which was in need of some TLC.

It was very plain and the sink pipes were exposed and there wasn’t any flooring laid.

But seconds later, Danielle shared a quick clip showing off the new flooring and the new sink.

You could also see paint samples that had been painted onto the white wall, as Danielle decided which colour would suit the small space best.

Following this, we then saw Danielle doing her very own DIY panelling.

Once this was done, she decided to opt for a beautiful beige colour and then got to work on the painting.

At the end of the clip, Danielle showed off the finished result.

The DIY fan opted for a modern herringbone flooring and had created her own panelling on each wall.

The toilet was completed with a variety of black accessories, including a black mirror, a black bin, a black rug and even a black toilet roll holder.

Not only this, but Danielle completed the room with faux flowers, a lovely diffuser and a candle.

Danielle explained that she bought her new sink and cabinet from Amazon and it was originally white, but she painted it black to suit her aesthetic. 

She also revealed that she used a Dulux paint in the shade Egyptian Cotton and nabbed her mirror from Dunelm. 

Danielle’s new toilet not only looks very modern, but stunning too, so it’s no surprise that her clip has impressed many. 

It has quickly amassed 64.5k views and has racked up 4,457 likes, 41 comments and 49 shares.

Social media users were eager to take to the comments to praise Danielle’s epic overhaul.

One person said: “I love it so much!!!”
