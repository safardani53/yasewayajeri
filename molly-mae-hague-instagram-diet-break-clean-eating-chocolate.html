Molly-Mae Hague breaks clean eating diet for chocolate bombe at The Ivy with Maura Higgins and Tommy

August 2024 · 3 minute read

MOLLY-Mae Hague has broken her clean eating diet in the best possible way - with a melting chocolate bombe.

The Love Island star headed to the stylish The Ivy restaurant, in Manchester's Spinningfields district, for a slap-up meal with boyfriend Tommy Fury and best mate Maura Higgins.

The trio started off with cocktails at the al fresco section of the eatery, with Molly, who has admitted to keeping a small, close friendship group, captioning their 'cheers' with the words: "My people."

After capturing images of their arancini balls and starters, the influencer honed in on the final course.

She filmed chocolate sauce being poured on top of a chocolate bombe, which promptly melted to reveal an ice cream middle.

Molly can be heard saying "thank you" to the waiter for the delightful dessert before she added the caption: "Omgggg."

The Filter brand founder rounded off the night in good spirits after a gift from boxer Tommy.

She posted an Instagram slide showing her holding a bunch of red blooms and wrote: "Ending the night with some surprise flowers.

"I love you Tommy Fury."

Earlier this month, Molly-Mae couldn't resist breaking her four-month 'clean' eating plan when catching up with Maura, who she hadn't seen in months due to the recent national lockdown.

The pair feasted on a 'special chocolate cake' filled with caramel almond and vanilla custard at London's exclusive Zuma restaurant.

Molly-Mae's indulgent day of eating comes after she changed up her diet and at the start of the year revealed her weekly healthy meal plan.

The influencer was cruelly trolled over her figure last summer while on holiday with boyfriend Tommy, and she has since changed up her lifestyle.

Despite being in great shape at the time, Molly announced weeks after the trip that she had started a strict diet.

In a vlog post not long afterwards, Molly told how she was giving her lifestyle an overhaul - but insisted it had nothing to do with the trolls.

She said: "I’m on a really strict diet plan at the moment.

"For breakfast I’m going to be having some cereal. I’ve been having the same breakfast for the last month. It is 30 grams of Coco Pops – an exciting breakfast."

Making light of the tiny portion, she said: "That’s like a normal portion guys. I must have been having about 300 grams before I knew 30 grams was a normal portion."
