Joe Thomas Biography

Joe Thomas was one of the best players during his school and college career. He was picked by Cleveland Browns in the first round of 2007 NFL draft. Browns was offered the contract of $ 43 million and made an NFL debut against Pittsburgh Steelers. He later signed a seven-year $ 84 million contract with the same club in 2011.

Thomas is the only offensive lineman to be named to the Pro Bowl for the first eight seasons. He was also voted as an 18th best player among the top 100 players of 2014. He received Pro Football Focus’ Bruce Matthews Award for best offensive lineman in 2015. Thomas has been named to 10th straight Pro Bowl in 2016.

Joe Thomas is married to the former University of Wisconsin’s basketball star, Annie Thomas. The couple got married in the summer of 2007 and has celebrated their 9th anniversary.

The couple first met each other during their college days at the University of Wisconsin. They are blessed with two daughters, Camryn and Logan and are happy together.

Thomas holds an American nationality and is white by ethnicity. The offensive tackle star has been earning a huge amount from salary and endorsements. His net worth as of 2014 was estimated to be about $48 million and is expected to have increased widely at present.
