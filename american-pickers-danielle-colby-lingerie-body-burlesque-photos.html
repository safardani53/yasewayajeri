She explained: "Shooting again, in this beautiful acid green glass bead 1920s gown that I had painstakingly rehabilitated by Jans in Michigan a few years back.

TOO LOW?

The History Channel series has since struggled to keep its viewership up as they've dipped drastically lower than before, with previous seasons reaching over $1million viewers per episode.

Season 24 of American Pickers premiered on January 4 and ran weekly until March 8. 

The remainder of Season 24 returned on July 5 and ran until August 23. 

The U.S. Sun reported that the August 23 episode brought in the lowest numbers for the season, with about 620,000 viewers.

After that, the network made unannounced schedule changes and has not aired any new episodes.

Around the same time, a friend of her co-star Mike Wolfe's told The U.S. Sun that he and brother Robbie were considering retiring from the show.

“I don’t know how long the show will last. I don’t know how long Mike and Robbie want to do this. I think they would like to retire at some point. It’s been 12 years.

“It’s a strain on your life," the pal said.

Robbie became a co-host on the show after taking over for former star Frank Fritz, 57, following his firing in July 2021.
