Jeannie Mai Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

May 2024 · 1 minute read

Bio:
Jeannie Mai (born Jeannie Camtu Mai on January 4, 1979 in San Jose, California) is an American makeup artist and TV personality. She started her career at age 18 as a makeup artist for MAC, and eventually begin working with celebrities, including Christina Aguilera and Alicia Keys. Eventually, Jeannie began auditioning for TV hosting gigs, and booked jobs on Stir and The Daily Mixx. Her first primetime hosting role was in 2005, on USA Network’s Character Fantasy, and in 2009, she took over as host on Style Network’s How Do I Look? Jeannie began hosting Miss Universe in 2011, and in 2013, she became a co-host on The Real. She appears as a fashion expert on several well-known talk shows, and has also worked as a correspondent for Extra. In 2015, Jeannie dropped out of hosting the Miss USA pageant due to Donald Trump’s anti-Mexican remarks in his 2015 presidential campaign.

Best Known For:
Jeannie is best known for host How Do I Look? and being a co-host on The Real.

Personal Life:
Jeannie married Freddy Harteis on Aug. 11, 2007.
