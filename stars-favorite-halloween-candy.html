Stars' Favorite Halloween Candy

MILEY CYRUS

The Hannah Montana star would trade to get her treat. "All I wanted was a Twix. I was like, 'Can I go through your bag and get me a Twix out?' That's all I really like."

ASHLEE SIMPSON

The singer loves a Halloween classic: "The candy corns! Christmas is peppermint, and Halloween is candy corn."

NEIL PATRICK HARRIS

The How I Met Your Mother star loves Reese's Peanut Butter Cups."There's nothing better, it's like an opiate," he says. "the more you eat them, the more you have to eat them – until you burst!"

MICHAEL URIE

The Ugly Betty star goes for tradition. "Candy corn, because it's the color of Halloween," says Urie. "And they're tasty – they're all sugar!"

T.R. KNIGHT

The Grey's Anatomy star loves Pixy Stix – even though they can be trouble. "I really like those a lot," he says, "Except when you take too much and you cough and [the powder] comes out."

KATHERINE HEIGL

For Halloween, Snickers are just what the doctor ordered. Why? "Because it's chocolate!" says the Emmy-winning Grey's Anatomy star.

TRACY MORGAN

The 30 ROCK funnyman has a sweet tooth, admitting to a love for mini-Snickers. "It's chocolate, it's peanuts – it's Snickers!"

ANA ORTIZ

"I haven't trick-or-treated in so long," jokes the Ugly Betty star. Still, she remembers her favorite treat from her childhood. "The mini-Crackel, in the red wrapper from Hershey's! That's a good one."

ZACHARY QUINTO

What's the only thing that can stop Heroes's evil Sylar? "Skittles!" says Quinto. "I could eat them until I explode."

JAMES DENTON

"I'm not a big candy corn guy," admits the Desperate Housewives star. So what's his weakness? "The little mini Snickers [are] awesome!"

EMMY ROSSUM

Peanut MampM's and Jolly Ranchers are the actress's hard-earned childhood favorites. "I grew up in New York City, so door-to-door trick or treating is really dodgy," she says. "You never know what you're going to get."

RAINN WILSON

Much like Dwight on The Office, Wilson turns indulging in his favorite treat, candy corn, into an organized project. "You can bite out the colors one at a time," he says.

WAYNE BRADY

The Don't Forget the Lyrics host is mad about Peanut MampM's. "I like to bite the candy shell and eat the nut on its own. If you see a dish with all the nuts missing, you know I was there. I'm the nut bandit. That would sound really weird out of context."

AMANDA BYNES

The Sydney White star is "nuts" for a good treat. "My favorite Halloween candy would be a cross between Reese's Peanut Butter Cups or Peanut Butter MampM's," she says. "Anything peanut butter."

JERRY SEINFELD

"You'll think I'm saying this to be funny," says the Bee Movie star, but "my favorite Halloween candy really is Bit-O-Honey. It's always been Bit-O-Honey. If you go into Dylan's Candy on Third Avenue in Manhattan, you'll see my name is up there with a picture of me with Bit-O-Honey, because that's my favorite."

STEPHEN COLBERT

From truthiness to toothiness: "I like the little candy corn. I like to bite the tip that's white off, the middle that's kind of brown, and then eat the orangey end – in that order," says The Colbert Report host.
