Kardashian Jenners flaunt glam gowns for Christmas Eve party- but key sister is missing from new vid

March 2024 · 3 minute read

KYLIE Jenner and three of her sisters have been featured in a new video where they showed off their Christmas Eve outfits.

The 26-year-old was the first Kardashian sister to show up in the video, and she was flaunting her curves in a gold dress.

Kylie's long-sleeve and off-the-shoulder gown was covered in sparkles as it hung to her thin body and shrinking waist.

The famous momager, Kris Jenner, 68, was the next family member to appear, and her dress matched Kylie's.

She wore a long down with a sparkly gold halter top and a black skirt flowing from her waist to the floor.

Kris was the person who posted the video on her Instagram Story.

Mother of two Khloe Kardashian, 39, appeared next while wearing a tight champagne color dress that was covered in rhinestones.
