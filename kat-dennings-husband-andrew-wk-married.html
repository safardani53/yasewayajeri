Who is Kat Dennings husband, Andrew W.K.?

October 2024 · 3 minute read

TWO Broke Girls star Kat Dennings revealed she was engaged to her partner Andrew W.K. in May 2021.

Now the two have tied the knot and fans want to know more about Kat's husband, Andrew.

Who is Kat Dennings’ husband, Andrew W.K.?

Kat Dennings is married to Andrew W.K.

Andrew Wilkes-Krier, also known as Andrew W.K., is an American singer-songwriter born May 9, 1979, in Stanford, California.

Andrew had his big break in March 2000 when he released his EP Girls Own Juice.

The following year, in 2001, Andrew signed with Island Records releasing his first major-label studio album I Get Wet.

Since his big break, Andrew has released six studio albums including his last 2021 album God Is Partying.

Due to his success, Andrew has a reported net worth of £3.183 million ($4 million USD) as per Celebrity Net Worth.

When did Kat Dennings and Andrew W.K. get married?

On Monday, December 11, 2023, Kat Dennings revealed in an interview with Vogue that she married her partner Andrew W.K. on November 27, 2023.

She revealed the two tied the knot in a small ceremony at their Los Angeles home.

Speaking on the wedding, Kat said: "I wanted our wedding to feel completely different from an ‘industry event’ in every way.

"I even handmade all of our wedding florals, aisle pieces, and our ceremony arch, which took three entire days. I wanted to be hands-on with the entire experience and feel like we had built our moment together from scratch."

Kat and Andrew began dating at the start of 2021.

The two dated for months before announcing they were engaged in May 2021.

In an Instagram post sharing the big news, Kat flashed her ring with the caption: "Don't mind if I do."

Speaking on her engagement on The Drew Barrymore Show in February 2022, Kat said: "I was like, 'This is my person. This is it. I can't live without him. This is the man I'm supposed to be with.'

"And we looked at each other, and we both proposed at the same time."

Who else has Kat Dennings dated?

Throughout her career, Kat Dennings has been romantically linked to an array of celebrities.

Some of her most notable relationships include Criminal Minds' Matthew Gray Gubler which she dated briefly in 2007.

She also dated her Marvel co-star Tom Hiddleston in 2010. However, the two called it quits before the 2011 Thor premiere.

Kat dated her Two Broke Girls co-star Nick Zano from 2011 until they ended things in 2014.

Lastly, Kat was romantically linked to singer Josh Groban.

The two were introduced by her Two Broke Girls co-star Beth Behrs in 2014.
