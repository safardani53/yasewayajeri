Joe Jonas and Blanda Eggenschwiler: It's Over!

April 2024 · 1 minute read

Attention, young women of the world:

Joe Jonas is now single!!!

The artist’s rep has confirmed to Us Weekly that Joe and Blanda Eggenschwiler have ended their 20-month romance.

“Joe and Blanda have mutually decided to part ways,” reads the statement. “The split was amicable and the two remain friends, with great respect for one another.”

About a year ago, of course, The Jonas Brothers broke up themselves. Many fans still cannot process the news.

Joe began dating the graphic designer and illustrator in November 2012, a few years after he ended things with Ashley Greene. (Jonas has also been with Taylor Swift, Camilla Belle and Demi Lovato.)

He and Eggenschwiler, a former model, were introduced through a mutual acquaintance.

Talking to VH1 at Lollapalooza over the weekend, Jonas eyed his future and said he’s “focusing on music, acting and writing” his own material. We wish him luck.
