Sierra &amp; Cheyenne- Wife &amp; Son

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Chris Jericho has twin daughters, Sierra and Cheyenne Irvine, nicknamed Sisi and Chey, respectively.

Christopher Keith Irvine, widely recognized by the ring name Chris Jericho, is an American-Canadian professional wrestler and rock musician.

On October 2, 1990, Chris, as Cowboy, professionally debuted at the Moose Hall in a ten-minute time draw against Storm.

During the 1990s, Chris, aka Lionheart Jericho, wrestled for the American organizations Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) and World Championship Wrestling (WCW).

Additionally, in 1999, the wrestler debuted in the World Wrestling Federation (WWF). Later, in 2001, he became the first undisputed WWF champion.

Furthermore, Jericho is a six-time world champion and held WWE Intercontinental Championships nine times.

Moreover, he was also the fourth Grand Slam champion in the history of wrestling.

Currently, he is signed to All Elite Wrestling (AEW), where he is the leader of the Jericho Appreciation Society stable.

Sierra & Cheyenne: Daughter Of Chris Jericho

Chris Jericho has one son named Ash Edward Irvine (born: in 2003) and twin daughter named Sierra Loretta Irvine (“Sisi”) and Cheyenne Lee Irvine (“Chey”). The twins were born on July 18, 2006.

The twins used to have a YouTube channel together where they uploaded vlogs and videos. It was called Sisi and Chey Vlogs and Videos.

Their subscribers and followers count grew rapidly after Chris talked about the channel in his podcast, Talk is Jericho.

However, it seems the YouTube channel is closed right now, and they don’t use it anymore.

In one of the podcasts, the girls also mentioned that the Irvine family loves Star Wars movies, and Star Wars has made their bond stronger.

The podcast happened after The Force Awakens was released in December 2016.

Additionally, Sisi and Chey revealed that they are not fond of wrestling and unwilling to pursue that career.

The children only watch the WWE if Chris is playing and get upset when the wrestler loses.

How Did Chris Jericho Meet His Wife?

Chris Jericho is married to Jessica Lockhart (born: August 30); the couple tied the knot on July 30, 2000.

The couple met at a Japanese restaurant in Tampa, Florida. At that time, Jericho was committed to World Championship Wrestling.

Moreover, it was love at first sight for Cowboy. But she had to leave for a three-week trip to northern Minnesota the day after they met.

However, they continued to stay in touch via phone. After his return from Minnesota, they eventually started dating and later exchanged their vows.

Furthermore, showing Jericho’s love for her, he tattooed an homage on his ring finger.

Son: Ash Edward Irvine

Ash Edward Irvine, son of Chris Jericho, was born on September 25, 2003. Edward is currently building his career in football.

Moreover, Irvine’s oldest son is also an expert in fishing, and he has a theme of the ocean in his room.

The footballer had mentioned that when he was young, he wanted to study sharks and fish and wanted to become an ichthyologist.

Furthermore, he loves everything about the underwater world and wants to become a marine biologist.

Chris’s son wrestled in high school and won two wrestling matches. However, Ash doesn’t want to follow in his father’s footsteps and become a wrestler.

The son is also seen regularly in the podcast “Talk is Jericho.”
