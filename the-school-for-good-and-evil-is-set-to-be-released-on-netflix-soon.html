The School for Good and Evil Is Set To Be Released On Netflix Soon

April 2024 · 3 minute read

The School for Good and Evil, Netflix’s next fantasy film, is set to premiere on the platform on Wednesday, October 19, 2022, at 12 a.m. PT. The film is directed by Paul Feig, who is best known for films such as The Heat, Bridesmaids, and Ghostbusters, as well as TV shows such as Freaks and Geeks and Other Space. It follows two closest friends whose once unbreakable friendship is tested when they are abducted.

Sofia Wylie and Sophia Anne Caruso play the primary parts, with others playing significant supporting ones. The film is based on the book of the same name by acclaimed novelist Soman Chainani. David Magee and Paul Feig wrote the script.

What to Expect, The Trailer, and More Details for Netflix’s The School for Good and Evil

Netflix released the official trailer for The School for Good and Evil on September 13, 2022, which opens with a menacing voiceover that states,

“Every so often, a very fortunate outside applicant is chosen for entrance to this revered institution.”

The trailer provides a peek at the film’s magnificent and mystical setting. It depicts the mysterious school where the two main protagonists are abducted and develop the basic narrative without disclosing too many important facts that may spoil the watching experience. According to Netflix’s Tudum, the plot of the film is as follows:

“The film follows best friends Sophie (Sophia Anne Caruso) and Agatha (Sofia Wylie) as they are whisked away into an enchanted school where aspiring heroes and villains are educated to safeguard the balance between Good and Evil.”

According to the narrative and trailer, viewers can expect an exciting fantasy drama that delves into a variety of complicated subjects such as evil, morality, and friendship. Fans of The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina and Shadow & Bone will undoubtedly appreciate this film.

More information about the actors of The School for Good and Evil

Sofia Wylie and Sophia Anne Caruso play Agatha and Sophie, respectively, in The School for Good and Evil. Both women look fantastic in the trailer, and their onscreen chemistry is one of the film’s strengths. The two principal actors are expected to provide outstanding performances. Sofia Wylie has been in many notable productions, including Andi Mack, High School Musical: The Musical: The Series, and Shook. Back of the Net, a comedy directed by Louise Alston, also featured her.

Sophia Anne Caruso has appeared in a number of TV episodes and films, including Jack of the Red Hearts, Evil, and Smash. She has also performed extensively in theater, where she is most recognized for her roles in Lazarus, The Nether, and Beetlejuice. Charlize Theron, Laurence Fishburne, and Michelle Yeoh, among others, play crucial supporting roles with Anner Caruso and Wylie. Don’t forget that The School for Good and Evil will be available on Netflix on Wednesday, October 19, 2022.
