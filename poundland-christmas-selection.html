I went to Poundland to check out their Christmas selection - I couldnt believe how good it was

June 2024 · 2 minute read

IF you're still working your way through your Christmas shopping list, then why not head to Poundland.

One shopper has recommended visiting your local cut-price store to pick up some gifts for your loved ones – or even a treat for yourself. 

TikTok user Mrs Clare Hoops, who can be found at @mrsclarehoops, recorded herself hitting the Poundland aisles, as she encouraged her followers to come shopping with her. 

As she walked through the popular discount store’s doors, she said: “Let’s have a look at what’s in Poundland for Christmas.”

Clare then went through the things that had caught her eye, as well as revealing what she eventually ended up buying for herself. 

First, she pointed out a Christmas cookie jar that came in the shape of a Christmas tree for £5. 

Next, she said that the various festive mugs, which are £3, would make a great gift option.

They come in a variety of designs, including a Christmas tree and a Christmas pudding. 

She was also a big fan of the £1 reusable "jumbo sacks" which she said would be a “really nice alternative to gift bags” – and just as cheap.

Other items that Clare liked included Christmas tree plates for festive snacks, Christmas lights, and “vintage style” green and red reindeer ornaments.

But there were several things that made it into her basket, starting with a small wooden sleigh, which she said was going home with her. 

She also bought a mini Christmas tree shaped chalkboard that was only £1.

Clare explained: “I only came to have a look and I’ve gone home with a cookie jar, a tray, some little chalkboards and some ties for the garland.”

She added in the caption: “Poundland, you didn’t disappoint me for your Christmas gifts and goodies.”
