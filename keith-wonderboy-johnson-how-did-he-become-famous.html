Who was Keith 'Wonderboy' Johnson and how did he become famous?

July 2024 · 3 minute read

LEGENDARY gospel singer Keith "Wonderboy" Johnson has reportedly passed away at the age of 50 on Friday, September 30, 2022.

The tragic news was confirmed by Black Gospel Radio before being confirmed by heartbroken friends who took to social media to share their poignant tributes.

Who was Keith Johnson?

Keith Johnson was an American gospel singer-songwriter, who went by the stage name ''Wonderboy''.

Born on May 17, 1972 in Brooklyn, New York, Keith was first given the nickname ''Wonderboy'' at the age of five thanks to his wonderfully talented vocals.

In his earlier years he started singing with his father Philip Johnson and uncles as a member of The original spiritual voices, before forging a successful solo career.

The news of Keith Johnson's passing was first announced by Black Gospel Radio before being confirmed by heartbroken friends.

How did Keith Johnson become famous?

The Brooklyn-born musician first rose to fame in 1998, when he started his solo music career with the release of his first gospel album Through the Storm.

Over the course of 13 successful years, Keith released 14 albums with a number of different record labels, including World Wide Gospel Records, Worldwide Records, Verity Records, Central South Records, and Blacksmoke Records.

Impressively, 11 of his 14 albums charted on the Billboard magazine Gospel Albums.

His other works also landed on the Independent Albums and Heatseekers charts. 

And recognition for his talents didn't stop there - the 50-year-old also bagged six Stellar Awards and nominations for two Dove Awards during his career.
