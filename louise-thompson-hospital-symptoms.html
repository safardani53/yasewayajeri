Louise Thompson back in hospital as she shares worrying symptoms that wont go away

February 2024 · 2 minute read

LOUISE Thompson has revealed she is back in hospital as she shared some of her worrying symptoms that won’t go away.

The brave ex Made In Chelsea star was previously diagnosed with PTSD and has been documenting her health journey after the traumatic birth of her son Leo.

She shared a photo from her hospital bed and said she was booked in for a procedure but was terrified.

Louise, 32, penned over the photo: "Trying to avoid feeling triggered, but my body keeps on dropping in temperature and shaking, almost like I'm in shock.

"It's amazing how the body clings onto trauma. Let's get this done and restore my faith in hospitals.

"V keen to override my fear of general anaesthetic and the fear of never waking up again."

Louise, who is engaged to fiancé Ryan Libbey, thanked the NHS staff following the birth of Leo after she "nearly died" during labour.

She had to stay in hospital for a month after her C-section and hailed "the room full of people who worked through the night to save my life".

The Instagram influencer added at the time: "To dance with death twice brings a new view of the world. A stark reminder of how short and sacred life is."

Her health issues have been ongoing since and she has been in and out of hospital, and taking medication for anxiety, since.
