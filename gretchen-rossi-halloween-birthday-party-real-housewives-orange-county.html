February 2024 · 3 minute read

GRETCHEN Rossi decided to make the most of having a Halloween birthday by throwing an epic birthday bash on Thursday.

The Real Housewives of Orange County alum, 42, kept her fans up-to-date with the celebrations as she shared an array of snaps on Instagram.

Gretchen looked incredible as she completely transformed her look for the lavish dinner party which had a very obvious Halloween theme.

The blonde star looked unrecognizable as she wore a long black wig, a sexy red dress and had her face painted with a Day of the Dead theme.

As she posed for photos in her spooky attire, a lavish dining table could be seen in the background - complete with gigantic floral arrangements, a spiderweb table cloth and skull head candles.

Gretchen captioned the post: "Had the best time at my Halloween themed birthday party!

"Thank you to my wonderful bestie @lisaarasheben & all my beautiful girlfriends for throwing such an a spooktacular bash for me. It was truly epic!

"Thank you to the @redorestaurant & all your amazing staff for taking such good care of us!"

She continued: "Absolutely exquisite flowers we’re done by @eucharis.studio, & the amazing crow tree balloons arches were created by @the.balloonbox."

"We all love a good theme party and it was so much fun to dress up this year for Halloween!

"Thank you to my incredibly talented makeup artist @foreveruntamed for creating exactly the look I wanted! #dayofthedead #Halloween #red #costume #redo Día de Muertos #diadelosmuertos."

Fans were quick to comment on the post, with one person writing: "Wow how truly beautiful your party is so glam & a touch of spooky at the same time LOL."

A second follower added: "You look awesome," while a third commented: "You are so pretty."

Gretchen and her friends dined like Halloween queens at Mexican restaurant RED O restaurant and even sipped on their drinks using black wine glasses.

As well as enjoying bottles of wine, the ladies also downed shots of tequila and were heard cheering as the waiter approached their table.

"Yeah! We’re doing shots in the middle of the day! I like it!" Gretchen can be heard saying in a video she posted on Instagram.

However, it seems she wasn't feeling too hungover for her actual birthday on Friday and continued the celebrations with another epic transformation.

This time the mom of one dressed up as the Queen of Hearts and looked super glamorous in a satin gown complete with a gold crown.
