Brooklyn Beckham and fiance Nicola Peltz strip naked for cheeky mirror selfie

April 2024 · 2 minute read

BROOKLYN Beckham posed for a daring topless selfie with fiancée Nicola Peltz yesterday.

The 22-year-old branded the actress, 26, his "absolute world" in the sizzling mirror shot that he shared to his Instagram Story.

In the skin-flashing picture, Brooklyn is seen behind the Transformer star while his right arm is wrapped around her.

Despite the pair being shirtless in the selfie, Nicola cleverly covered her modesty by holding up a scarf as they posed in their white-walled hallway.

Captioning the attention-grabbing shot, Brooklyn penned: "My absolute world.

"You are my best friend and my soulmate @nicolaannepeltz".

Brooklyn's tribute on his Instagram Stories comes a week after Nicola was struck down with a mystery illness.

The note was signed off with both Victoria and David's names, as well as their children's - including oldest son Brooklyn, who Nicola is engaged to.

Nicola gushed alongside the post: "I have the best family, I'm so lucky - thank you so much!"

The blonde beauty did not clarify what she is poorly with, but was glammed up for an event earlier this week.

Recently, Nicola was accused of raiding her fashion designer mother-in-law's wardrobe after Victoria spotted her wearing one of her brand's £750 bustiers.

Victoria shared a snap of the star posing in the top to her Instagram, cheekily adding: "Looks like the future Mrs Beckham has been in my wardrobe again!

"But you're forgiven because I love this VB bustier on you @nicolapeltz."

Returning the compliment, Nicola replied in the comments: "Caught! I love you so much."

Fans were thrilled by the exchange - saying that having chic Posh as a mother-in-law would be any girl's "dream come true".
