Holly Willoughby's leather skirt and red jumper wins more fashion fans as Instagram followers rush t

January 2024 · 2 minute read

HOLLY Willoughby has scored another fashion win with her latest This Morning outfit - and fans have been desperately snapping it before they miss out.

Holly, 36, sent her followers into a frenzy after opting to wear a red frilled jumper from Very and black leather skirt from Jigsaw for Monday's appearance on the daytime show.

The blonde TV star completed her look with a pair of black ankle boots from Topshop and it all appeared too much for fans.

After sharing several snaps of her outfit on Instagram, Holly had racked up an impressive 42,000 likes in just two hours.

She was also inundated with hundreds of positive comments, with many fans telling her they had quickly raced online to buy her exact outfit.

One fan commented: "Love your jumper Holly, I’ve just ordered it before it goes out of stock."

Another of Holly's fans gushed: "I love your style! Jumper just ordered x."

Other followers told how they were frantically trying find Holly's ankle boots, with one messaging her friend: "I'm trying to find out which Topshop boots. Or style name?!"

Holly's red jumper has a price tag of £32, and has already sold out in sizes 8 to 14.

Her Jigsaw skirt has a slightly heftier price of £150, and the size 12s have already gone.

MOST READ IN TV & SHOWBIZ

It is fair to say that Holly has become a total style icon of late, impressive fans with both her outfits on This Morning and Dancing On Ice.

During Sunday night's instalment of the skating show, the mum-of-three wowed in a multi-coloured sequin dress.

The frock was from the designer Attico, however it is priced at an eye-watering £1,320 albeit reduced from £2,200.
