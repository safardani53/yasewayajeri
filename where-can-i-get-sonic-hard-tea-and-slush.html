When is SONIC Hard Tea and Slush being released?

COOP Ale Works announced SONIC's Hard Southern Tea and Slush will hit retail stories in September of 2022.

What is the alcohol content of the drinks?

The SONIC Hard Southern Tea will have an ABV of 5%.

The SONIC Hard Slush will have an ABV of 6%.

SONIC Hard Southern Tea will be sold as a 12-pack in 12 oz cans or as a single 24 oz can.

The drinks will only be available to purchase at stores, not at SONIC Drive-In locations.

You can find a store near you that sells SONIC's new hard drinks via their website.

What flavors do the drinks come in?

The SONIC Hard Southern Tea will be a black tea with a hint of lemon.

The SONIC Slush will come in three flavors, Cherry Limeade, Blue Raspberry, and Watermelon.
