Who are the cheapest players in Football Manager 24?

May 2024 · 6 minute read

FOOTBALL Manager 24 was released today, and players have been eager to dive into the action.

Whether you are playing FM24 on Netflix, console, or PC, there are ways to build up your team for almost nothing.

If you’re diving into the action right away, then here are some players that you can grab for free or cheaply.

Here’s the lists of all the free, cheap, and best-value players in Football Manager 24.

Who are the cheapest players in Football Manager 24?

The cheapest players in FM24 are free agents, or players whose contract with another club has just expired.

These are usually players who were demanding a higher salary or could have had a lot of injuries in the last season.

Just because the above players are free it doesn’t mean that they are the best value, as they may not show clear results.
