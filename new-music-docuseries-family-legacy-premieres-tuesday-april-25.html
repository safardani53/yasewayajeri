New Music Docuseries, Family Legacy, Premieres Tuesday, April 25

October 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Premiering this spring, the series gives new perspective to legendary moments in pop culture history.

Ready to see a whole new side of your favorite musical legends?

Family Legacy, a music docuseries premiering exclusively on Paramount+ on Tuesday, April 25, provides an all-new, intimate look at artists including Linkin Park, Van Halen, Backstreet Boys, NSYNC, Boyz II Men, TLC, Biggie Smalls, Sean "Diddy" Combs, Brandy and Melissa Etheridge from the POV of their children.

SUBSCRIBE: Sign Up For Paramount+

The five-episode series is narrated by actor and music artist Quincy Brown and combines exclusive MTV archival footage with brand-new interviews, taking fans on a tour of unforgettable musical moments with untold perspectives and stories.

Features in the series are:
- Andrew Hagar (son of Van Halen's Sammy Hagar)
- Bailey Cypheridge (daughter of Melissa Etheridge)
- Baylee Littrell (son of Backstreet Boys' Brian Littrell)
- Briahna Fatone (daughter of NSYNC's Joey Fatone)
- Christian "King" Combs (son of Sean "Diddy" Combs)
- C.J. Wallace (son of Notorious B.I.G.)
- Draven Bennington (son of Linkin Park's Chester Bennington)
- Justin Combs (son of Sean "Diddy" Combs)
- Nathan Morris II (son of Boyz II Men's Nathan Morris)
- Quincy Brown (son of Sean "Diddy" Combs)
- Sy'Rai Smith (daughter of Brandy)
- Tron Austin (son of TLC's Rozonda "Chilli" Thomas)

Stay tuned for more information on Family Legacy at ParamountPlus.com!
