Love Islands Molly Marsh makes huge change to social media as she breaks silence on TikTok after

March 2024 · 3 minute read

LOVE Island star Molly Marsh has made a big change to her social media after breaking silence on TikTok after her shock axe.

Following her shock exit from the villa last Friday, 21-year-old performer Molly has had tongues wagging about whether she’ll go back into the villa.

And she’s since returned to social media, diving head first back into her life pre-Love Island and making TikToks with her younger sister.

The star jumped online today to post a dance video alongside younger sister Maisie, where they re-enacted a viral sound from the girl group this series, which repeats: “It’s giving sexy.”

Molly donned a green jumper and white trousers as she grooved beside her sister.

She wrote: “When you are reunited with your dance partner…”

But in a new move on her socials, Molly has disabled all of her comments, meaning fans aren’t allowed to write anything beneath her posts.

Prior to this, the blonde beauty had comments turned on and allowed fans to write messages and responses to her posts.

Molly returned to Instagram too, as well as TikTok, and shared a string of stories, from fan comments to her appearance on Aftersun this past weekend.

She also posted a sweet snap hugging her sister Maisie, after the pair were reunited.

It comes after Molly’s co-star George Fensom hinted that she’d gone on the show ‘for the fame.’

In a recent interview, George was asked who was on the show for the Instagram blue tick.

He said: “I’m not hating on the girl but obviously Molly is an influencer, that’s what she does for work.

“So you’re always going to think, and that’s what I always thought, are you in here to boost yourself up?”

George continued to tell Access All Areas: “I suppose whether you’re a scaffolder or an influencer you can go in there for love and that’s what the show is for.” 

Molly had to hit back at claims she was a “producer plant” after entering the show, after fans realised she’d already visited the Love Island villas in the past.

A source revealed: “Molly was scouted after visiting the villa in South Africa earlier this year.

“She was on the tour as an invited guest when she caught the eyes of producers.
