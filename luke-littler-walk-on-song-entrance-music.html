Luke Littler walk on song: What is the entrance music that World Darts Championship sensation uses?

May 2024 · 2 minute read

LUKE LITTLER has taken the World Darts Championship by storm.

The 16-year-old sensation is playing in the final against World No 1 Luke Humphries as he eyes the £500,000 prize.

Littler began his quest with a win over Christian Kist, and then Matt Campbell in the third round.

It was then in the fourth round when he defeated five-time world champion Raymond van Barneveld.

All eyes are on Ally Pally now as the pre-tournament favourite Humphries awaits.

What is Luke Littler's walk on song?

Luke Littler has had Ally Pally in the palm of his hand, and it has started with his bouncing entrance song.

Littler walks on to Greenlight by Pitbull featuring Flo Rida and LunchMoney Lewis.

The 2016 hit was the fifth single off Pitbull's tenth studio album, Climate Change.
