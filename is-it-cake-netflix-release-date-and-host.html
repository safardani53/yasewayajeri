INTERNET trends are coming to the small screen especially with Netflix's new show Is It Cake?

Is It Cake? from Netflix brings the cake trend from social media to life with a reality baking competition series.

When does Is It Cake premiere?

On March 7, 2022 Netflix released the trailer to the new baking show on YouTube.

Viewers will be able to stream the series beginning on March 18, 2022.

The show will see an array of celebrity judges appear on the show to answer the ultimate question: Is It Cake?

Some of the celebrity judges seen in the trailer include SNL's Heidi Gardner, comedian Fortune Feimster, and more.

most read in tv

The show will have a group of bakers creating real edible cakes that appear to be other objects that are not cake.

The grand prize according to the trailer will be $50,000 and will go to the top three bakers.

Who will host Is It Cake?

The trailer also revealed who will be hosting the new baking show.

Saturday Night Live's Mikey Day is set to be the main host for Is It Cake? come March 18.

Day joined the show SNL during its 39th season according to his bio on the NBC site.

He will be the one asking the guest judges to guess whether or not the cake they see in front of them is an edible cake or an object.

What is the cake trend the show is based off?

Netflix is capitalizing off the internet trend that went viral in 2021.

The trend saw people posting images to Twitter and Instagram of cakes that did not appear to be cakes.

These cakes looked so much like the object they were created to appear like that once they were cut open it blew people's minds.

The bakers of the series include Andrew Fuller, April Julian, Dessiree Salaverria, Hemu Basu, Jonny Manganello, Justin Rasmussen-Ellen, Nina Charles, Sam Cade, and Steve Weiss.

"What I do is the weirdest thing in the world," Manganello said in the trailer.

"Like, there are people saving lives and I am making cake look like other things."
