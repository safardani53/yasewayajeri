14 Best Nude Bras 2020 | The Sun UK

June 2024 · 7 minute read

THE NUDE bra is the un-sung hero of your wardrobe - and we mean it when we say, every woman needs one.

This essential underwear item is the key to making the most of your clothes, but only if it fits perfectly.

The perfect nude bra should look and feel invisible. It creates a smooth silhouette and blends into your skin tone - ideal for wearing underneath your staple white T-shirt or the daring sheer dresses sported by Kim Kardashian.

Contrary to popular belief, a nude bra looks better underneath white clothing than white underwear, which can be easily mismatched and often greys over time due to washing and general wear.

And while it may be true that underwear that looks better with your clothes on does not look so good with your clothes off, we've sussed out a few hybrid beauties that tick all of the boxes.

Here's our round-up of the best nude bras that you can buy no matter what your size, shape, style and the occasion.

1. Best nude bra for bigger busts: Fantasie's Smoothing T-Shirt Bra

  • DD+ T-shirt bra, £34 from Fantasie at Figleaves - buy here

Those with bigger busts may struggle to find a nice streamlined bra that doesn't have all sorts of hoisting mechanisms and ultra-wide straps. Fantasie's nude T-shirt style has moulded cups with minimal padding to create a smooth, natural-looking shape but the full cups provide support and coverage, so you can avoid spilling out of your top.

2. Best nude bra for choice of shades - Nubian Skin

  • Nubian Skin classic lace push-up bra (pictured), £39 from House of Fraser - buy here
  • Nubian Skin strapless convertible bra, £34 from House of Fraser - buy here

There's long been a call for the beauty industry to challenge what 'nude' actually means (hint: it's a lot more than just 'beige') but thankfully in 2020, there are some amazing brands out there offering shades to suit a variety of skin tones.

Nubian Skin is one of them—and just like Fenty Beauty's iconic foundation, this lingerie line has shades aplenty, so you'll never feel mismatched again.

They've even got a skin tone guide to handily match your shade, based on the foundation you wear.

The classic lace bra is a great place to start and features gel padding to accentuate your curves and a pretty floral lace design.

3. Best bandeau nude bra - Accessorize's Bandeau Bra

  • Bandeau bra, £12 by Accessorise from Very - buy here

Let's face it, bandeau bras are useful, but they tend to err on the ugly side. Accessorise has come through with a pretty and practical version that's a lingerie drawer must-have: made from a breathable jersey, it's set on a pretty lace band that sits smooth on your skin and has removable padding, so you can switch up the silhouette.

It's completely wire-free and did we mention that it's super comfortable too?

4. Best bargain bra - Nude Lace Trim T-Shirt Bra

  • Nude lace trim t-shirt bra, £5 from Peacocks - buy here

Peacocks's T-shirt bra is trimmed with lace, so it's prettier than most basic styles. The cups are moulded and lightly padded to create a rounded silhouette and give a subtle lift. It's our favourite bargain buy at just £5. You can't argue with that!

5. Best triangle bra - Organic Basics's Organic Cotton Triangle Bra

  • Organic Cotton Triangle Bra, £36 from Organic Basics - buy here

This is Organic Basics's best-selling organic cotton bra for a reason: there are no wires, but plenty of support.

If you love the fuss-free approach of an unpadded bra but need something that holds you in, this style is ideal—it goes up to a size XL, which one reviewer describes as: 'great for my DD/E Cup and no sweating, as the fabric is breathable and oh so soft!'

It's also a brilliant option if you're looking to make more eco-friendly choices too, as it's made fairly in Europe with no nasty chemicals.

6. Best for breastfeeding - PANACHE's Eleanor Moulded Nursing Bra

  • PANACHE Eleanor Moulded Nursing Bra Latte, £40 from Bra Stop - buy here

Nursing bras are not always the prettiest but, with its smooth cups and lace detailing, this Panache version is a diamond in the rough. It has subtle drop-down clips for easy feeds, provides support with its full-coverage cups and still looks streamlined underneath your clothing. Oh, and did we mention it comes with convertible clips so that you can make it racer-back?

7. Best push-up bra: Wonderbra

  • Push-up bra, £24 from Wonderbra at Debenhams - buy here

Trust Wonderbra to make the best of your bust. The nude ‘Full Effect’ bra is a good one to have in your repertoire - the brand claim it will make your look two cup sizes bigger. Crafted with a mixture of air, gel and featherweight foam, it attacks your assets from all sides to give you the ultimate lift.

8. Best T-shirt bra: Microfibre T-shirt bra

  • Microfibre T-shirt bra, £12.99 from H&M - buy here

Your ultimate wardrobe hero, a T-shirt bra is named as such because it should look invisible underneath the tight-fitting jersey of a simple T-shirt. It should be smooth, not too padded and well-fitting, so that it sit close to the breast but doesn't create any undesirable bulges. H&M's is made of microfiber fabric which wicks moisture away from the body and holds its shape, even after multiple wears and washes.

9. Best multiway bra: 100 ways to wear bra

  • 100 ways to wear bra, £22.50 from Marks and Spencer - buy here

A party girl's best friend, this nude bra by Marks and Spencer isn't called '100 ways to wear' for nothing. Cleverly constructed with deconstruct-able cups and straps, you can contort and convert it to suit any outfit - racer-back, one shoulder, strapless, backless, plunging and more. It's the best £22.50 you'll ever spend.

10. Best looking: Silk-blend bra

  • Silk-blend bra, £27.50 from Rosie for Autograph at Marks and Spencer - buy here

Made from shell coloured silk and creamy lace, this Rosie by Autograph bra is the nude bra exception. And the best thing is, it is insanely comfortable too; the sling construction is supportive and creates a natural-looking silhouette that looks virtually invisible underneath your clothing - a rarity for lace underwear. Buy the matching briefs and you'll won't have any unfortunate Bridget Jones moments, ever again.

11. Best sports bra: Uniqlo

  • Wireless bra, £14.90 from Uniqlo - buy here

Uniqlo's sports bra may not be the prettiest, but it's guaranteed comfort. High impact movement makes your breasts bounce, which can cause sagging and stretching over time - so it's really important to keep everything supported when you're exercising. Uniqlo's has bonded seams which sit completely flat to the skin and inbuilt, moulded cups that enhance your natural silhouette so you look smoking hot in your gym kit...just don't show anyone what's underneath.

12. Best stick-on bra: ASOS

Stick-on bras aren't a new concept, but the separated adhesive cups of old didn't exactly make the most of our assets. Enter the lace-up version, this clever bra works in the same way but has moulded butterfly cups and a corseted front so that you can create and control your own cleavage. Perfect for when you're going all guns blazing in a low cut, backless number.

13. Best sustainable bra: H&M

  • Non-wired bra, £17.99 from H&M - buy here

From H&M's coveted Conscious Exclusive collection, this nude bra is spun from recycled nylon to create a beautiful lace fabric. Cut into a cool triangle shape, its crafted without underwiring or padding to achieve a natural-looking silhouette. It has pretty spaghetti straps too, so you can channel Kendall Jenner and let it show beneath your clothing.

14. Best all-rounder: Calvin Klein

  • Iconic push-up bra, £36 from Calvin Klein at Zalando - buy here

There's a reason that Calvin Klein has been a go-to underwear brand since the 1990s. This nude number is our a absolute all-rounder for everyday wear and super comfortable. It's set on a stretch-mesh band that sits flat to the skin and has supportive cups with bonded seams that look invisible underneath your clothing. The gradual padding creates a nice little lift that is appropriate for everyday and it has those branded logo-stamped straps that we all know and love.
