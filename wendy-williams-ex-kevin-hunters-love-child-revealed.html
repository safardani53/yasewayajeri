Wendy Williams' ex Kevin Hunter's 'love child' revealed for first time as he steps out with 'mistres

August 2024 · 5 minute read

WENDY Williams’ ex-husband Kevin Hunter's love child has been spotted for the first time- two years after a cheating scandal broke up their marriage.

In The Sun's exclusive photos, Wendy's former partner and executive producer holds his toddler daughter during an outing near the new Florida home he shares with his alleged longtime mistress, Sharina Hudson.

The Sun can report that Kevin, 48, Sharina, 36, and their daughter Journey, 2, are living together as a family unit in the affluent community of Coral Springs - hundreds of miles away from his ex-wife and the flashy life he once lived as talk show host Wendy's husband.  

On June 8, Kevin and Sharina ran errands together with their daughter.

The NYC producer turned Florida-based dad was spotted carrying his little girl and a tote bag to their car.

Later, Sharina was pictured going to a local nail salon with a friend.

On June 10, Kevin, Sharina and Journey ventured out on another family outing.

Kevin, driving a Ferrari, and Sharina, out in an Audi, arrived at another home in the area to visit friends.

In one snap, Sharina lovingly gave her daughter a kiss, and in another Kevin affectionately reached out for the tot’s hand as they made their way down the sidewalk.  

In other shots, Sharina toted a bag with writing on it that cheekily read: “You fake like this Birkin.” 

During their outing, Kevin was seen outside enjoying a smoke break.

The photos show the father of two living a subdued and domesticated life.  

A source exclusively told The Sun that Kevin is in a good place two years after his scandal with Wendy, 56.

The insider said: “He is living with Sharina and their baby. 

"They're just really happy together, they have moved on from all the drama in New Jersey with his marriage.

"It's done wonders for their relationship.” 

The divorce could have been much explosive, the source insisted, had Kevin- the father of Wendy's son Kevin Jr, 20- decided to speak out publicly at the time.  

The source added: “Kevin is a very quiet, strong man.

"This thing could have exploded like war of the roses in public if he started speaking out.

"He really kept quiet, and he was doing the right thing for his son's sake."

While Kevin’s life appears to be in harmony, his happiness isn’t easy for his ex, the source told The Sun.  

“He's happy and that's a tough pill for Wendy to swallow, especially since she hasn't found someone yet,” the source claimed.  

Wendy is currently single after a split from businessman Mike Esterman.  

The source told The Sun: “It's tough being Wendy and dating in such a bright spotlight. She is having a hard time.

"Meanwhile, Kevin has always been so private. Which is the complete opposite of Wendy who gossips publicly for a living.” 

The Sun has also learned that Kevin has opened up a barbershop in nearby Boca Raton, Florida, called Head Hunters – after his last name, Hunter.  

The shop is another indication of how different his life is following his high-profile job at the Wendy Williams Show.

Wendy and Kevin went through a messy breakup in 2019 after it was reported that his alleged lover Sharina had become pregnant with his child.  

The TV host, who has been open about her struggles with alcohol and substance abuse in the past, appeared to spiral out of control after the scandal. 

She reportedly checked into rehab in Florida. 

Wendy returned to New York from her rehab stint and moved into a sober house in early 2019, the TV host admitted.

After it emerged that Sharina gave birth in March that year, Wendy filed for divorce from her husband of more than 20 years. 

The pressures of their divorce were further amplified when their son, Kevin Jr, got into an alleged altercation with his father in a parking in New Jersey.

The 20-year-old was subsequently arrested for simple assault – a charge that would ultimately be dismissed. 

Wendy later claimed she knew Kevin had a "side girl" for almost 15 years of their nearly 22-year marriage.

She said on The Jess Cagle Show: “I've known about her almost since the beginning. I've known that Kevin is a serial cheat.

"The first time I found out was while was pregnant with our son on bedrest. During that time I gave birth to him, he was in the delivery room."

Wendy went on: "I plotted to divorce Kevin, and I sacrificed a lot of myself to come out successful on the other side. And it all worked."

"This girl wasn't the only one. She just happens to be the one who kept his baby. I said 'kept his baby' because from what Mr Hunter told me on the night that she was with this new baby Journey and her family and whatnot down south, he was with me begging forgiveness."
