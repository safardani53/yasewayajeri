Elsa Arrested For Causing Cold Weather

In the gallery above, you can see Queen Elsa freezing over a fountain of water into ice right before the police caught her! They cuffed her and threw her in the police car which drove off to the local station in Hanahan, SC.

Of course, this was all a joke in response to the towns freezing cold temperatures this month!

Just last week, the Harlan police also placed the blame on Queen Elsa for their cold temperatures! Poor Elsa — the cold doesn’t bother her but it bothers us!

The Harlan police posted this bulletin on their Facebook:

“APH has issued an arrest warrant for Queen Elsa of Arendelle. Suspect is a blonde female last seen wearing a long blue dress and is known to burst into song ‘Let it Go!’ As you can see by the weather she is very dangerous. Do not attempt to apprehend her alone!”

We love this adorable idea — making an uncomfortable situation into a fun joke! In the pics above, you can see the police actually let Elsa go after brining her in — does this mean the cold temps aren’t over just yet?!
