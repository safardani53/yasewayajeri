THE Greatest Snowman will air this Christmas Eve with a celebrity line up.

The brand-new snow-show will welcome five celebrities but who are they?

Who will be competing in The Greatest Snowman?

Laurence Llewellyn-Bowen

Laurence is famed for appearing on the DIY home improvement show Changing Rooms, which aired on BBC from 1996 to 2004 and got revived on Channel 4 in 2021.

He has frequently appeared on DIY SOS: The Big Build as one of the designers.

Outside of designing work, Laurence has presented for radio channel Classic FM and made guest appearances on various game shows.

Dani Dyer

Daughter of Danny Dyer, Dani rose to fame when she competed in the 2018 season of Love Island.

She and her ex partner Jack Fincham won the show and got their own reality show Jack & Dani: Life After Love Island.

The pair have since split.

Dani rekindled with an ex-partner from before her Love Island days, Sammy Kimmence, and the pair have a child called Santiago, who was born 23 January, 2021.

Liam Charles

Liam took part in the 2017 season of The Great British Bake Off, coming fifth overall.

Due to his popularity on the show, Liam was invited back to present Bake Off: The Professionals.

He is also a judge on Junior Bake Off and has written two cookery books.

Cherry Healey

Cherry is best known for her light-hearted documentaries which appear on BBC Three.

Typically, the documentaries are self-titled and will look into various subjects such as dating, money and drinking.

She has also participated in Celebrity MasterChef in 2016.

Johnny Vegas

Johnny Vegas is a well-known comedian and actor.

The funnyman can be seen in the British sitcom Benidorm and BBC Three comedy Ideal.

He also makes frequent appearances as a panellist in 8 Out Of 10 Cats, 8 Out Of 10 Cats Does Countdown and Celebrity Juice.

When is The Greatest Snowman on TV?

The Greatest Snowman will air on Channel 4 on Christmas Eve, (24 December, 2021).

The show will air at 8pm and will be available on Channel 4's streaming service All4 immediately afterwards.
