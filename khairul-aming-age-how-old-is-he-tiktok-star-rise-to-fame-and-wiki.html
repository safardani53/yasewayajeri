Khairul Aming Age: How Old Is He? TikTok Star Rise To Fame And Wiki

Khairul Aming Tiktok Star: The Malaysian social media sensation got the 2023 Content Creator Of The Year Award, and Wikipedia has been buzzing online. Khairul Aming, actual name Khairul Amin Kamarulzaman, is a prominent Malaysian TikTok creator.

Aming has a significant fan base because of his culinary videos and distinctive product, Sambal Nyet. On TikTok, he has over 3.6 million followers and 53.6 million likes. He rose to fame after winning the Creator of the Year award at the 2023 TikTok Awards Malaysia. As a consequence, many people are curious about both his personal and professional life. Here’s what we know thus far.

Khairul Aming Wiki: How Old Is The TikTok Star?

Khairul Aming is a Malaysian TikTok celebrity who just received the TikTok Awards Malaysia 2023 Creator Of The Year Award. In terms of age, the TikTok celebrity is in his early thirties. On August 28, 2023, the event was hosted at MITEC in Kuala Lumpur. He began his internet career eight years ago by filming modest videos, such as cooking on a chair. Eventually, the brilliant maker rose to prominence throughout Ramadan by sharing several traditional dishes with his fans.

Khairul Aming has over 3.6 million TikTok followers and his studio and factories are located in Kota Bharu. He is also the creator of Sambal Nyet, a hot sauce brand that he sells both online and offline. Many Malaysians who aim to be content producers and entrepreneurs are inspired by Khairul Aming. He has shown that one may realize one’s ambitions via hard effort, enthusiasm, and innovation while also positively impacting society.

Khairul aming’s Patience and Commitment Story

Khairul Amin Kamarulzaman attended Vanderbilt University in the United States after graduating from SMK Ismail Petra in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. At the university, he studied mechanical engineering. At the age of 21, he purchased his first DSLR camera in order to learn more about photography and cinematography. Aming was on the dean’s list every semester and graduated with his class. He began as a rotational engineer before moving on to a pipe engineer role.

At the age of 24, he stepped beyond his comfort zone to work in logistics for his industry, where he learned about the supply chain and the global market. He had a full-time job and a few side hustles. After experiencing his first taste of reality, the social media sensation returned to his core skill and passion, filmmaking and cuisine. In a year, he built his following to 500K and attempted to offer them his own Sambal. Using his money, he managed everything from R&D to logistics. However, his product was a failure.

Khairul Amin used his RasaPop experience to create cooking films that reflected his personality and eccentricity. Fans of the TikTok celebrity @KhairulAming and his brand adored him. His Ramadhan “30 hari, 30 resepi” series was a smash success. According to reports, the film has received millions of views on his YouTube channel. Early in 2021, the TikTok star released his second offering, “Sambal Nyet Berapi.”

It was a big success, raking in RM 1 million in three months. His tweet about it received 73K likes, demonstrating his fans’ support. In a year, he expanded his company to RM 14 million and added a second location. Khairul Aming was unsuccessful, yet he did not quit. He worked hard, remained dedicated, and waited patiently. The TikTok celebrity transformed his hobby into a lucrative company. Nonetheless, his ten-year journey has been incredibly inspirational.
