Niall Horan 2024 tour: How can I get tickets?

July 2024 · 4 minute read

SINCE leaving One Direction, Niall Horan has launched a successful solo career.

His solo career includes three albums and an upcoming 2024 tour.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

When is Niall Horan going on tour?

On May 23, 2023, Niall Horan announced a new tour on his social media pages.

"I am beyond thrilled to announce The Show Live On Tour 2024 ! It’s been far too long and I can’t wait to see your beautiful faces," he said at the time.

The former One Direction member will be hitting the road in February 2023 to play shows around the world through July.

How can I get tickets?

Tickets for Horan's tour are scheduled to go on sale to the general public starting at 10am local time on June 2, 2023.

Presale tickets are also available for Citi and AAdvantage on May 30 and for newsletter members on May 31.

There will also be a TikTok presale on June 1, according to Horan's announcement.

Tickets can be purchased on Ticketmaster or on Horan's website.
