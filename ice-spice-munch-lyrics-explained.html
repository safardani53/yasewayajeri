What is a munch? Ice Spice's lyrics explained

September 2024 · 2 minute read

RAPPER Ice Spice has made headlines for her smash hit "Boy's a liar Pt.2", but another song's lyrics has left some fans scratching their heads.

"Munch (Feelin U)" includes vocals that have a New York slang term, "munch", throughout the song - what does it mean?

What is a munch?

Ice Spice, whose legal name is Isis Gaston, released her single, "Munch (Feelin U)", on August 10, 2022.

Since the song's release, fans have been clamoring to know what "munch" means as it's referred to in the song.

The hook in the song is: “You thought I was feelin’ you? That n**** a munch.”

Coming from the Bronx music scene in New York City, Ice Spice's song first went viral on TikTok before making its way to the radio.

It reached the No. 5 spot on Billboard's Top 100 singles and has been played on radio stations repeatedly.

The lyric "munch" in Ice Spice's song is a slang term for performing oral sex on a woman without getting anything in return.

According to Urban Dictionary, the term is for a man who is used to giving oral sex without receiving anything in return.

The site says a munch is: "A eater, or a super simp who do anything for a girl off the thought that she is feeling him."

What is the reaction to the song?

Ice Spice's song, "Munch (Feelin U)" has received unwavering support from her fans in the rapping community.

It skyrocketed to top the Billboard charts and reached over 13million listens on Spotify, quickly making it the go-to song of the summer into fall.

Her song received support from Drake, who reportedly flew her to Toronto where they were spotted at a concert together.

Drake praised Ice Spice's track and went on to promote it on OVO Sound Radio.

"That ‘Munch’ and your ‘On The Radar’ freestyle hard,” Drake messaged Ice Spice. He added: “Imma play em on our radio show.”

What other songs has Ice Spice released?

Ice Spice has 3million monthly listeners on Spotify, with "Munch (Feelin U)" making the top five of her popular songs.

Her number one song is her feature on PinkPantheress's track "Boy's a liar Pt.2".

Following the song people can't stop talking about, her popular songs include:

  • "Boy's a liar Pt.2" with 162million listens
  • "In Ha Mood" with 42million listens
  • "Munch (Feelin' U)" with 57million listens
  • "Gangsta Boo" with 19million listens
  • "Bikini Bottom" with 31million listens
