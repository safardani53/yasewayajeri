Stacey Solomon Got Married To Her Long Time Boyfriend Joe Swash

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Stacey Solomon, an English singer, married her longtime boyfriend Joe Swash in a small ceremony. According to the Daily Mail, the couple, who have been together for seven years, said their “I do’s” on July 24 at their Essex home in front of close friends and family. Stacey Solomon, who is Jewish, spent her special day in the back yard of their home, Pickle Cottage, which was decked up in fairy lights.

Stacey Solomon’s Jewish heritage

As previously stated, the 32-year-old is Jewish and regularly practices his faith. Her father, photographer David Solomon, was born in Poland and raised in Iraq as part of a Jewish family that immigrated to the United Kingdom. Regarding her Jewish roots, the singer stated:

“Growing up, Judaism instilled in me an overwhelming awareness to love people around you, care for others as if they were part of your family, and try to be the greatest person you can be.”‘

Solomon disclosed in 2020 that her mother, Fiona Solomon, a nurse, had converted to Judaism from Christianity in order to marry her father. Fiona was the daughter of a Church of England clergyman, according to the news website The Famous People. Stacey Solomon demonstrated how to build a Star of David using cinnamon sticks in an old Instagram story. She also said how much she values her Jewish background and how much her boys enjoy the event because of the presents.

“We celebrate ‘Chanukah’ with all other holidays, but the boys prefer ‘Chanukah’ because there are gifts.”

Stacey Solomon and Joe Swash experienced the best D-Day ever.

An informant told the Daily Mail that the couple enjoyed “the happiest day of their life.”

“It was everything they had dreamed for; the weather was perfect, and they spent the day with their children and family.” Stacey also wanted her day to reflect her strong Jewish beliefs while yet being contemporary and exciting for everyone. They had always wanted their wedding to be just for them, at their house, and their personal festivities did not disappoint.”

Linda Robson and Ricky Rayment, two of the bride’s co-stars, attended the wedding. The couple attended a romantic performance by James Argent’s ensemble, The Arg Band, and danced all night to music played by a DJ. The 32-year-old actress also disclosed on Instagram that the couple will have two ceremonies on their wedding day. She also said that they will have a “secret blessing” with their close family members before formally registering their marriage, according to Express UK.

Stacey Solomon and Joe Swash got engaged on Christmas Eve in 2020. The couple had intended to marry on July 31, 2021, but postponed their wedding. The couple already has two children, Rex (3) and Rose (1). (9 months). She is also a mother to Zachery (14), whom she shares with ex-boyfriend Dean Cox, and Leighton (10), whom she shares with Aaron Barnham. Meanwhile, Joe has a 15-year-old son, Harry, with his ex-partner Emma Sophocleous.
