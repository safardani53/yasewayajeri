M&amp;S shoppers rush to buy 'must-have' blazer and shorts worn by style icon Holly Willoughby

August 2024 · 2 minute read

SHE’S one of daytime television’s biggest stars and a style icon – with pretty much everything Holly Willoughby wears going on to sell out immediately. 

So it’s no surprise that her latest outfit from Marks & Spencer has been labelled a must-have and has sent fashion fans into meltdown.

This Morning star Holly recently modelled a stylish blazer and shorts matching ensemble from the popular high street store. 

The 42-year-old presenter, who has been hosting the ITV show alongside Alison Hammond, was photographed sitting down in a chair as she showed off the blue tweed two-piece.

The blazer, which featured statement buttons and cost £79, and high-waisted shorts look was completed with some casual white trainers.

And Holly couldn’t get enough of the daytime to evening wear outfit, as she told her Instagram followers all about it.
