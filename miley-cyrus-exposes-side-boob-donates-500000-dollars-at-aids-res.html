Miley Cyrus Exposes Side Boob, Donates $500,000 at AIDS Research Fundraiser in Los Angeles

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Miley Cyrus made her presence very strongly felt at the American Foundation for AIDS Research (AmfAR) Inspiration Gala in Los Angeles last night.

In more ways than one.

First, the singer apparently forgot her bra at home, walking the red carpet in a bizarre outfit that exposed the world to most of her boobs:

This is the second time in a few days that Miley has gone a bit X-rated for a good cause, previously grabbing her crotch in an effort to encourage men to be checked for testicular cancer.

But we can’t mock Miley too much in this case… because she also donated $500,000 to AIDS research at the event!

Cyrus spent $300,000 on a print of a naked woman by photographer Ryan McGinley (after getting into bidding war with Rihanna and Tom Ford) and then also donated another $200,000 on her own.

“I’m a little drunk. That’s why I’m a half a million deep,” Miley joked (maybe) from the stage before talking about her new role as MAC Cosmetics’ new Viva Glam celebrity spokesperson and adding:

“If I’m going to have a voice I would like to start an open dialogue about prevention and awareness and how people in the world have been effected by HIV and AIDS.

“Hopefully, I could break down people’s walls that have been built up around sexuality. I’m obviously not too embarrassed to talk about these things, especially with young people.”

Gotta give Cyrus nothing but props here, don’t you?

She even enabled Gwyneth Paltrow to be funny for a moment, as the gala’s co-host later took to the stage and remarked:

“Miley Cyrus has so much more money than I do. What the f-ck?”
