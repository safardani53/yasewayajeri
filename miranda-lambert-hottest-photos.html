May 2024 · 2 minute read

In honor of Miranda Lambert’s 40th birthday, take a look at photos of the gorgeous country star performing at concerts, walking red carpets, and more.

Miranda Lambert is a true star! The Texas native has become one of the most successful artists in country music. As of 2021, she has released 9 studio albums, all of which have been positively received by fans and critics. Some of her most notable singles include “The House That Built Me,” “Mama’s Broken Heart,” “Gunpowder & Lead,” “Little Red Wagon,” and “Bluebird.” Miranda has received a plethora of awards in the music industry, and she’s even been honored by the ACM Awards more than any other artist.

Outside of her career, Miranda had a high-profile marriage to Blake Shelton from 2011 to 2015. A few years after they split, Miranda got married to NYPD Officer Brendan McLoughlin in January 2019. The couple have been going strong and are so in love.
