Matthew Wade Biography

34-year-old Matthew Scott Wade was born in Hobart, Australia, on December 26, 1987. His father, Scott Wade, played Australian rules football for Hawthorn in the Victorian Football League (VFL), for Clarence and Hobart in the Tasmanian Football League (TFL), and held the position of CEO of AFL Tasmania for a considerable amount of time. Michael Wade, his grandpa, presided over the Hobart Football Club. Wade is the cousin of Jeremy Howe, a defender with the Collingwood Football Club.

Wade played junior football and cricket for Tasmania, serving as vice-captain of the Tassie Mariners in the TAC Cup while playing with future AFL stars Sam Lonergan, Grant Birchall, and Jack Riewoldt. At the 2006 ICC Under-19 Cricket World Cup, he represented Australia.
