Who Is Dess Dior? 5 Things To Know About The Rapper Hollywood Life

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Future and Lori Harvey are over for good and it seems that Future has already moved on. On November 22, the 37-year-old “Life Is Good” artist was featured on Dess Dior‘s Instagram Story, showing off his affection for his potential new flame. In the photo, which you can see below, a shirtless Future nuzzled Dess’ shoulder and placed his hand on her hip. The up-and-coming artist, 22, looked positively stunning, rocking a green top with one long sleeve and prominent cutouts with a pair of fierce pants. Although fans are talking about Future and Dess’ potential new romance, there is so much more to this young woman than meets the eye. Learn more about Dess Dior with the facts below!

1) She’s building her rap career. Dess and Future already seem to have quite a lot in common. The 22-year-old is working on building her burgeoning music career, and definitely has the talent. Dess has already released a 2020 EP entitled Definition Of Dess along with a slew of singles. Some of her biggest hits thus far include “NO Hook,” “Talk to Me,” and “Summertime.” Her music can be found on Spotify, Tidal, Apple Music, and YouTube.

2) Dess is also into fashion. Across her Instagram page, Dess has posted a slew of photos featuring her posed-up in some chic threads. Some of Dess’ photos have featured the young artist sporting Balenciaga, Chanel, and more. It also seems that Dess is getting into modeling, judging by some of the more professional looking photos on her IG feed.

3) She’s already working with beauty brands. Not only is Dess proving that she has exceptional taste in fashion, the artist has also shown off her favorite beauty brands for her many fans. Dess has worked with Pretty Melanin in the past, and she’s even posted a few videos on her Instagram page featuring herself using the products that she loves!

4) Dess is currently on a tropical getaway. The exact location of Dess and Future’s whereabouts is difficult to judge. However, it appears that the two are spending some time in a tropical location away from prying cameras. Recently, Dess’ Instagram posts have featured her posing-up by palm trees and on her Instagram Story, she went horseback riding on the beach.

5) She’s well-established on social media. Although the 22-year-old’s career is just getting started, she already boasts quite the number of followers on Instagram and on Twitter. On the former social media platform, Dess has roughly one million followers. On Twitter, Dess has over 290K followers. There’s no doubt that as her career grows, those numbers will, too!
