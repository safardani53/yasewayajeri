Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna Hit Strip Club, Make It Rain

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Those who watch Rob & Chyna online or on television each week have thus far seen Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna arguing.

A lot.

That’s practically all these two do on their new E! reality series.

But the expecting parents will take a break from fighting next Sunday in order to do something else:

Watch grow women take off their clothes and dance around on stage!

That’s right, as you can see in the clip above, Rob and Chyna are headed to a strip club!

Along with Blac Chyn’s mother (what? Huh? Yup!), Chyna takes her fiance to the same establishment where she once worked.

Indeed, Blac Chyna used to be a stripper. She’s come pretty far since then, hasn’t she? Sort of.

Despite the cadre of scantily-clad females in his presence, however, Rob will only be able to focus on the one carrying his baby next Sunday.

"I don’t ever want you going back here again," Rob tells Chyna in the club.

How sweet, right?!?

On this past Sunday’s installment of Rob & Chyna, the former opened up about his insecurities.

Does he often feel ashamed because he’s overweight? Yes.

But a negative experience with an ex-girlfriend didn’t exactly help matters, either.

"I had a really bad past relationship where the girl was cheating and having sex with so many different people," he said at one point.

Rob didn’t name names, but he didn’t have to. He was clearly referencing Rita Ora with this quote.

How can we be so certain? Because Kardashian has said in the past that Ora cheated on him with at least 20 dudes.

Watch Rob & Chyna Season 1 Episode 2 Online

We actually do feel pretty bad watching Rob on this television show.

Because it’s so horribly produced and clearly scripted and contrived? No. Not only for that reason, at least.

It’s just pretty clear that the guy really does have a lot of personal hang-ups. Yes, of course he chose to star on his own reality series. He’s choosing to put himself out here.

But it’s very easy to see him being pressured to do so by his family, most notably his mother and his fiancee.

There’s very little in Rob Kardashian’s past that makes us think he wants fame and attention, especially when so many storylines are focused on his weight and general sadness.

Are we truly losing sleep over Rob’s plight? No. We’re not crazy.

The guy is raking it in here.

But the lack of enthusiasm on his part to really want to be a celebrity permeates every episode. And just makes us hate his loved ones even more than we hated them previously.

Oh, and we did mention that Blac Chyna brings HER MOM TO THE STRIP CLUB, right?

Okay, good. We thought we did. But it bears repeating anyway.
