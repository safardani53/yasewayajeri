Packages for Maluma Land are expected to go on sale on April 20, 2022, starting at 12pm EST.

They will be available through the event's website, along with VIP upgrades.

There are a variety of packages that include hotel accommodations on the Las Vegas strip starting at $999 per person.

At this time, standalone tickets are not available, and you must be at least 21 years old to purchase a package.
