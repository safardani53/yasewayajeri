Teen Mom Mugshots Timeline: Jenelle Evans, Amber Portwood, More

February 2024 · 1 minute read

Multiple members of the Teen Mom franchise have run into trouble with the law.

While some, including Jenelle Evans and Amber Portwood, have posed for the camera on multiple occasions, other stars’ mugshots, including Farrah Abraham and Adam Lind, have not been released by authorities. (Abraham was first arrested for a DUI in Omaha, Nebraska, in March 2013, and Lind’s most recent arrest was in December 2017 after Chelsea Houska’s ex violated his probation for a no-contact order).

Us Weekly confirmed in January 2022 that Abraham had been arrested again after allegedly slapping a security guard outside a Los Angeles nightclub. She was taken into custody and was issued a citation before being released hours later.

Portwood, for her part, was arrested for domestic abuse against ex-boyfriend Andrew Glennon in July 2019. (She was released from custody several days later.)
