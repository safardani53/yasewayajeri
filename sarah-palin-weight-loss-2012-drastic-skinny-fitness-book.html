Sarah Palin Weight Loss Former Governors Scary Skinny New Body Hollywood Life

June 2024 · 2 minute read

The almost stick-thin Sarah looks a far cry from her normal, healthy self!

But a new tiny body isn’t the only thing the former Alaskan governor, vice presidential candidate and mother of five revealed. Sarah also shared that she is writing a fitness book.

“Our family is writing a book on fitness and self-discipline focusing on where we get our energy and balance as we still eat our beloved homemade comfort foods!” Sarah told People.

Sarah isn’t the only Palin who is a fan of fitness. Her oldest daughter, Bristol Palin, 21, is appearing on Dancing With The Stars: All-Stars and is noticeably losing weight while competing. Sarah is currently in LA to support her daughter while she appears on the popular ABC dance competition’s.

Despite debuting her skinny new look and announcing her fitness book, Sarah hasn’t given word on when the book will be published or if it has been contracted yet.

Nutrition expert Lisa De Fazio tells HollywoodLife.com exclusively, “Sarah Palin looks very thin! She appears to weigh about 105 lbs currently and 140 lbs previously. I am not sure how she lost the weight, however I hope she is eating enough! She appears somewhat healthy at this point, however I would not recommend she lose more weight…It is interesting that she is writing a fitness book! I hope she has medical experts and dietitians to contribute btheir expertise.”
